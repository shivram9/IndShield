Setup Your First Camera
  1. Open Manage Cameras
    Click on "Manage Cameras" in the sidebar menu
  2. Add Camera Details
    Click "Add New Camera" button and enter:
    • Camera ID: Use "0" for built-in webcam, or enter IP address (e.g., 192.168.1.100:8080) for network cameras
  3. Choose Detection Features
    Select which safety features you need:
    • Fire Detection: Monitors for fire hazards in real-time
    • Pose Alert: Detects emergency hand gestures (L-shape pose)
    • Restricted Zone: Alerts when someone enters prohibited areas
    • Safety Gear: Ensures workers wear proper protective equipment
  4. Save Configuration
    Click "Save" to apply your camera settings
  5. View Live Feed
    Return to this dashboard to see your camera feed with active detection
